WaterWaves1D.jl

Overview

WaterWaves1D provides a framework to study and compare several models for the propagation of unidimensional surface gravity waves (a.k.a. "water waves").

Several models are already implemented, included (but not limited to) the so-called water waves system, its truncated spectral expansion, the Green-Naghdi system, the Matsuno system, and so on. You may easily add your favorite one to the gang: see the how-to guide .

Installation

(v1.0) pkg> add https://github.com/WaterWavesModels/WaterWaves1D.jl.git
using WaterWaves1D
